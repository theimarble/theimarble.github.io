Introducing theMarble Site 2.0
22 December 2023


Welcome to theMarble Site 2.0!

This update is focused on improving the blog side of the site, as the old site wasn't well-suited to regular blogging.

The biggest change is blog posts are now seperate pages, with only the headers appearing on the home page, rather than the entire post like it was in 1.0.

An about page has also been added to help new viewers understand what this site is about and the music page has been redesigned.

A full list of changes is available on the site changelog.
