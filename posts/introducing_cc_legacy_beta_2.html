Introducing CC Legacy Beta 2
30 August 2024


Introducing CC Legacy Beta 2, the second beta of the world's largest Computer Clan archival and preservation effort.

This beta features CC Site 11.0 from 3 December 2013, along with the Computer Showdown, Ghost Murderer and Spoof OS sub-sites from that date. Note that the only the downloads section of the Spoof OS site works at the moment, this is due to some last-minute bugs that are currently being resolved.

To prevent issues with uploading such a large amount of content at once, content releases are scattered, so therefore only some of the downloads are available. On the upside, the heavily sought after Slide to Self-Destruct OST from Computer Showdown Season 6.0 is available in full.

Check out CC Legacy by clicking here, or clicking on the CC logo on the sidebar.
