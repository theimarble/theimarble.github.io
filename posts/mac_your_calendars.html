"Mac" your Calendars
October 24, 2024


Apple's SVP of Marketing Greg Joswiak today announced that Apple will be announcing new products starting next Monday. Strangely, this was only revealed through his personal X/Twitter account, rather than through more official channels, such as Apple Newsroom.

We're expecting a redesigned Mac mini with the M4 chip, along with refreshed 14" and 16" MacBook Pros with the M4 Pro/Max SoC and possibly a 24" iMac with the M4.

Personally, I'm looking forward to the 14" M4 Pro MacBook Pro, as I will be pre-ordering one as soon as they are announced.
