CC Legacy Feature Spotlight #1 - Dynamic Switching
16 December 2024


Dynamic Switching allows you to switch between CC site versions without exiting the currently active site, and also allows you to see how the CC site looked on a certain day, thanks to in-site content switching.

Note that Dynamic Switching is part of an internal development build of CC Legacy that will be released to the public in 2025.
