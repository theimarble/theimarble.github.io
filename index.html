My Top 5 Picks for WWDC '23

WWDC is only a few days away now, so here are my top picks before the event happens.
  • Apple will release a VR headset either named "Apple Reality" or "Apple Vision".
  • watchOS 10 will be the biggest update to the Apple Watch since its debut in 2015. Widgets similar to the iPhone will be added.
  • A 15" MacBook Air will be released, featuring the M2 chip. Starting price will be $1499.
  • Alongside macOS 14, the Mac Studio will be updated to feature the M2 Max and M2 Ultra chips. The starting price will stay at $1999.
  • Craig Federighi will mention Apple's crack marketing team when announcing macOS 13.
Overall, I'm super excited for this years WWDC, of course we will get the usual OS updates, but hopefully hardware will also be introduced this year too.
