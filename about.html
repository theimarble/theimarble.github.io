About

Hi!

I'm theMarble, and I run this place.

I love going in-depth into topics I'm passionate about, and this site will reflect that.

I also create my own wallpapers and music, of which is uploaded here.

Outside of that, I collect retro and collectible Macs that I find interesting, including a fully maxed-out 20" iMac G4, a Dual 2.7GHz Power Mac G5, and many, many PowerBooks and MacBooks.

You can find me on YouTube, where I occasionally post Marble Blast and Computer Showdown videos, Flickr, and soon on Mastodon.
