ABOUT CC TRIVIA

CC Trivia is a new, live, gameshow that is on CCL. You can compete and answer questions. When you do, you get a certain amount of points, and the points roll-over to other rounds. The person with the highest points at the end of the round gets the prize for that game.

There are seven questions delivered per game. The maximum amount of points a player can receive in a game is 24.

REGULATIONS

CC Members who play can still get points, but will not be rewarded.

Questions that are answered must be spelled correctly to receive credit for the answer. Rule may vary based on context.

We can only accept two answer submissions from each player. Only the most recent answer will be accepted.

The first correct answer gets 3 points, the second answer gets 2 points, and the third gets 1 point. These are round points, please read on to learn about the new game points system.

During the final question, the point values are doubled.

Points in the rounds will determine how many game points a player will receive. The game points are then rolled over to the next round. The player with the most game points at the end of the game is the winner.

1st Place in the Round: 5 Game Points

2nd Place: 4 Game Points

3rd Place: 3 Game Points

4th Place: 2 Game Points

5th Place: 1 Game Point

You may NOT fake other IRC accounts to attempt to win.

*Rules may change without notice. Check frequently.
