Episode 181 - Behind the Screens II

The new version of Final Cut Pro, and the brand new Mac Pro are making a big splash this week! In addition, we look at some upcoming LG products, we show the Apple Campus in Minecraft, and we have a lot of fun on IMDb. All of that, and more, in this great episode of CCL Podcast!


Episode 179 - Behind the Screens

Behind the Screens is a new subsection of CCL. Every so often we will be hosting a behind the scenes look at projects that Computer Clan members are working on. In this episode, we had several members working across Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, and Flash Pro. Along with that, we show other behind the scenes perspective of our projects, and we give some useful hints.


Episode 178 - Don't Copy That Floppy
