The Hardware House is a collection of photographs that are taken by Computer Clan members. The photos can contain anything that is tech related, and more specifically, hardware. The Computer Clan also runs the Software Showcase, which features screen shots of many different operating systems and software programs over time. Vintage Apple Tours is another series that features hardware, specifically, older Apple products. The series consists of short, and to-the-point videos that showcase the design and features of certain products. Feel free to enjoy these collections.
