COMPUTER CLAN GAME DIVISION



The game division is all about CC Game Studios. CC Game Studios is the production label for computer games that are made by the Computer Clan. Examples of these games are Windows Crap Edition, Project: Mission, Cyber Trap, and more. There are also many games that are in development, and they will be released in the future. YoYo Games provides us with a programs called GameMaker Pro, and GameMaker HTML5. We use both of those to create desktop, and browser games. We might be moving into development with Unity in the future for other 3D games.
