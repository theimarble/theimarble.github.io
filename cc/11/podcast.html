Episode 176 - Something in the Air

Bigger iPads, Porsche Design phones, Xbox Ones, iPad Airs, and more! What could possibly go wrong? Nothing, really.

Episode 174 - GooTube

The new YouTube commenting system, Steam Machine prototypes, LiveSurface Context, Garry's Mod gameplay, Minecraft Live, and a whole lot more! In addition, we had a very fun after show with quite the nostalgia trip!


Episode 173 - Blockhead

Phones are blocks, blocks are phones... wait, does that make sense? Motorola is making a modular smartphone, Google has the Nexus 5, and people can float in a balloon to space?
