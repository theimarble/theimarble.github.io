DIVISIONS

The Computer Clan has a variety of members. All have different skills and talents. These are the main divisions that the Computer Clan are split into. You can view the information for each division, see who's in it, and see what's going on. This might also give you an idea for what you would want to do if you choose to join.

GRAPHICS

Computer Clan members design graphics for multiple projects. YouTube thumbnails, 3D scenery, logos, banners, you name it. The Computer Clan utilizes graphic design in nearly all projects.


VIDEO AND MUSIC

The Computer Clan generates most of its traffic with videos and music. The Computer Clan's YouTube channel is available with this link. Check out that channel to view the kind of content we create. We're a variety channel, so you will notice short films, comedy bits, animations, tech demos, etc.

Our organization also writes and produces a lot of our own music for various productions, such as soundtracks. Our Music section can be found under Downloads.

DEVELOPER

The Computer Clan develops websites and mobile apps. We currently have an application called CC Central. It is a mobile application that let's users keep in touch with several aspects of the Computer Clan in an all-in-one mobile interface. We have a version for iOS and Android. Our iOS version is due for a major update. We also develop websites, such as the site you are on now, and the mobile version of the Computer Clan Site.

GAME

Over the years, the Computer Clan has developed dozens of games. The games of ours that are the most popular, are part of the Spoof OS series. These games are comical, interactive operating systems that can run on a desktop OS, or in a browser. You can see more games in the Games section under Downloads. We have removed a lot of our older games, but we still keep some of the more recent ones, especially for Spoof OS.
