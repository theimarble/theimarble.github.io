The Simple Man 1.0 for Mac OS X

Download



Overall features:

- 64 bit

- Simple copying interface

- Commands organized in tabs

- Definitions of what commands do


The Simple Man is a manual of Terminal commands for Mac OS X. Instead of reading man pages inside of a command line, you have nice UI to copy and paste commands into the Terminal.
