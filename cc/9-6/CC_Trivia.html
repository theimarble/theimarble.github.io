CC Trivia Regulations:

1. Questions that are answered must be spelled correctly to receive credit for the answer.

2. You may use any method to enter in your answer, but only the first two answers in the chat will be accepted.

3. The first correct answer gets 2 points, and the second answer gets 1 point.

4. The rounds will last for a certain number of weeks. The person with the highest score will receive the prize.

5. You may NOT fake other IRC accounts to attempt to win.

6. Your most recent answer entered in the chat is the answer we accept.

7. You may not answer more than twice.
